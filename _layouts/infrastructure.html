Soukhya Retirement Living

Infrastructure In VILLAS & SUITS

  • Fully furnished air conditioned bed room with twin bed.

  • Furnished drawing room.

  • Kitchenette with refrigerator,
    microwave oven & kettle.

  • Toilet with hot water and grab bar.

  • Balcony with view of full greenery.

KITCHEN

A modern open kitchen is provided in the main building. Participatory cooking is appreciated. Inmates can select food, from the choices provided on a daily basis.

DINING AREA

Restaurant like dining area is provided near the kitchen. Very good ambience for socializing along with food.

FITNESS CENTER

A well equipped fitness center is available with separate space for yoga and meditation

LIBRARY & READING ROOM

A well maintained library caters to the reading habits of inmates. News papers & periodicals are also available for casual reading

GAME FACILITY

A few indoor / outdoor games are made available for the effective use of time.

WALKWAY

Walkway is provided throughout the compound. Walking for exercise is possible inside the fruit garden also.

FRUIT GARDEN

Residents can enjoy a variety of healthy fruits from the organic fruit garden. It provides a serene, peaceful ambience for a morning walk and for spending evening time leisurely.

FULL TIME CARETAKER

Trained caretakers are residing in campus and are available round the clock.
