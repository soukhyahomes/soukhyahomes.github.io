Soukhya Retirement Living

Expert Care of the Elderly

RETIREMENT LIFE is the dawn of a new era for everyone. It is the phase of life to blissfully enjoy life’s pleasures without the pressure of responsibilities. But for many, it often winds up as a life of solitude and isolation. With children happily settled in their careers at faraway lands and friends of our yester years too far from reach, life becomes too secluded and lonely for elders. Many wishes and dreams yet to be fulfilled remain unattainable due to the lack of physical support or their age related health issues. Another major issue faced by the elderly today is security and safety as they are easy targets to criminals on the prowl. Life is all the more difficult if you are an elderly single without a companion. In short, the quality of life many of our elders are living today is miserable and tedious and they deserve better.

The idea of SOUKHYA VILLAs bloomed from these thoughts. This wonderful, luxurious elderly living facility is designed like a resort and is located near Kothamangalam in Ernakulam District, away from the maddening crowd of the city. Nestled beside a natural stream with panoramic scenic nature, it transports its residents to a realm of beauty and serenity. Additionally, airport and Kochi city are just 45 minutes’ drive from this location. It is also the perfect opportunity for the Non-resident Keralites who wish to make a second home in God’s own Country, to experience all its glory post their retirement.

Accommodation is provided in Luxury Studio Apartments / Villas with fully furnished air-conditioned bedroom with twin bed, furnished drawing room and kitchenette with refrigerator, microwave oven and kettle, toilet with hot water and grab bar and balcony with full view of luscious greenery.

Fully landscaped yard with walkway, an organic fruit garden, fish pond, separate parking area for residents and visitors, restaurant like dining area and food of your choice, well equipped fitness centre with separate zone for yoga and meditation, library & reading room, indoor and outdoor game zones, doctor & clinic, ayurveda wellness centre, gossip zone, laundry services and round the clock security.

Additional facilities like guest house for visitors, car with driver, on-call ambulance, tie-ups with major hospitals, lab and pharmacy support, nursing and wheel chair support, hospitalization, pick up and drop, travel & ticketing, treasury services, banking, purchases and shopping, sight-seeing, temple / church visits, regular entertainment programs and much more.

SOUKHYA VILLAs can be made your HOME or ALTERNATE HOME and be your key to living the most heavenly retirement life without even buying the property. Residents are chosen on the basis of a lifelong Membership program. There will be a returnable Onetime Security Deposit and nominal Monthly charges.

Through this program, members can stay here for their whole life time. There will also be an option for terminating the agreement in case any resident decides to discontinue the facility during their tenure.
