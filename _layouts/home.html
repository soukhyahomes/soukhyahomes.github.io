COMMUNITY LIVING FOR SENIOR CITIZEN
Retirement is the beginning of a new phase in life aiming at serene, calm and peaceful atmosphere. In soukhya villas it is envisaged to develop community living for senior citizens with all luxury and safety measures.

Why Choose Soukhya Homes?

A modern, safe, and vibrant community for your golden years

Amazing Atmosphere

Situated in the middle of a panoramic surrounding. Calm ambience with singing of birds, fruit trees, green natural lawn and also with a big pond having full of fancy fishes.

Latest Medical Care

Daily visit of a doctor is arranged. Soukhya has signed MOU with two multi-speciality hospitals. The residents can reach the hospitals in 15 minutes by car.

Vehicle & Driver

Car and driver is available in the campus for emergency services, 24 x 7. Also made available for any personal planned travels like airport pickup and drop, picnic etc.

Yoga for Relaxation

Yoga performing place is provided. A yoga trainer will visit occasionally.

Assisted Living Support

Soukhya will arrange full time assistance for the needy inmates.

Fulltime Caretaker

Caretaker is residing inside the campus for making their service available always.
