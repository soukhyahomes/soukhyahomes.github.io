Welcome to Soukhya Homes

Soukhya Retirement Living

Our Vision

Far off from the madding crowd amid panoramic scenes comfortable stay with community life style for those destined to isolation and frightening solitude.

COMMUNITY LIVING FOR SENIOR CITIZEN Retirement is the beginning of a new phase in life aiming at serene, calm and peaceful atmosphere. In soukhya villas it is envisaged to develop community living for senior citizens with all luxury and safety measures.

LOCATION 'Soukhya Villas' meaning comfortable village life is beside a natural stream at Kottappady near Kothamangalam in Ernakulum district with easy access to Airport, Railway Station and High ranges. It is surrounded by wonderful scenes with Nature's healing touch. Be with Nature and one with Nature at Soukhya Villas.

SURROUNDING

  • Sufficient parking area for residents, guests and visitors.
  • Fully landscaped yard with walkway developed like a resort refreshes mind.
  • An organic fruit garden developed offers inmates opportunity to enjoy nurturing of trees and plucking fruits.
  • Inmates can relax besides a fish pond and enjoy feeding fishes
  • A gossiping area also provided to relax in the free times of evening
