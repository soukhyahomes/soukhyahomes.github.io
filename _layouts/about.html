Soukhya Retirement Living

Our Vision

Far off from the madding crowd amid panoramic scenes, Soukhya offers a comfortable stay and community lifestyle for those seeking peace and companionship.

Retirement is the beginning of a new phase in life, aiming at a serene, calm, and peaceful atmosphere. At Soukhya Villas, we foster community living for senior citizens with all luxury and safety measures.

Located beside a natural stream at Kottappady near Kothamangalam, Ernakulam, Soukhya Villas is easily accessible and surrounded by nature's healing touch. Be with nature and one with nature at Soukhya Villas.
