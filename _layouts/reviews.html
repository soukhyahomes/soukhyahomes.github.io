Bala C Nair & Indira Nair

Myself and my wife are engineering graduates from CET, Trivandrum, lived many years abroad and moved to Kerala a couple of years back. Since children are away, we wanted to settle our last phase in a retirement home. After searching many, we settled at Soukhya Homes, Kothamangalam. This is our feedback about the finding at Soukhya Homes.

Soukhya Homes: A Tranquil Retirement Haven
Nestled in the serene village of Kothamangalam, Soukhya Homes offers an idyllic retirement living experience surrounded by the beauty of nature. The retirement home is set amidst lush greenery, featuring big trees, ponds, and expansive rubber plantations that provide a peaceful and refreshing atmosphere.

Accommodation and Infrastructure
Soukhya Homes comprises a few buildings that house both villas and apartments, catering to various preferences and needs of its residents. The apartments are designed for comfort and convenience, with spacious drawing rooms and bedrooms equipped with high-quality furniture and modern infrastructure. This ensures that residents can enjoy a comfortable and stylish living environment.

Facilities and Amenities
The community is well-equipped with a range of facilities aimed at promoting health, wellness, and social engagement. These include:
 • Gym: A well-maintained gymnasium to help residents stay fit and active.
 • Party Room: A space designated for social gatherings and celebrations.
 • Cinema Hall: An in-house cinema hall for movie screenings and entertainment.
 • Ayurvedic Treatment Facilities: Access to traditional Ayurvedic treatments and therapies for holistic well-being.
 • Common Dining Hall: A spacious dining area where residents can enjoy their meals together, fostering a sense of community.
 • Open Kitchen: An open kitchen where meals are prepared, allowing for transparency and involvement in the cooking process.
 • Library: A well-stocked library with a wide selection of books, newspapers, and magazines for the residents to enjoy.

Culinary Delights
The dining experience at Sokhya Homes is one of its highlights. The menu is curated based on monthly suggestions from the residents, ensuring a variety of delicious and nutritious meals. Both vegetarian and non-vegetarian options are available, catering to diverse tastes. The daily meal schedule includes buffet-style service:
 • Morning Tea: Served at 6 am to start the day.
 • Breakfast: Served at 8 am with a variety of options.
 • Lunch: Served at 1 pm, featuring wholesome dishes.
 • Evening Tea: Served at 4 pm, accompanied by snacks.
 • Dinner: Served at 8 pm, providing a fulfilling end to the day.

Activities and Community Engagement
Soukhya Homes offers numerous activities that foster a vibrant and engaging community life. Residents can participate in:
 • Yoga Classes: Regular sessions to promote physical and mental well-being.
 • Rummy Club: A place for card game enthusiasts to enjoy and socialize.
 • Gardening: Opportunities to engage in gardening, allowing residents to connect with nature and pursue their hobbies.
The community also celebrates most festivals, reflecting the rich cultural heritage of Kerala. These celebrations bring residents together, creating a joyous and festive atmosphere.

A Connection with Nature
One of the most cherished aspects of living at Soukhya Homes is its close connection with nature. The verdant surroundings and tranquil environment provide a perfect backdrop for a relaxed and fulfilling retirement life. Whether it's taking a leisurely walk among the trees, tending to a garden, or simply enjoying the scenic beauty, Soukhya Homes offers an unparalleled living experience for those in their golden years.

പൊതുവെ എല്ലാത്തിൻ്റെയും പോസിറ്റീവ് സൈഡ് കാണുവാൻ ശ്രമിക്കുന്ന എനിക്ക് പുതിയ അന്തരീക്ഷം , പുതിയ ചുറ്റുപാട്, ജീവിത്തിൽ ഒരിക്കൽ പോലും കണ്ടിട്ടില്ലാത്ത ആളുകളുമായുള്ള സൗഹൃദം ഇവ കൂടുതൽ കണ്ടെത്തി നൽകുകയാണ്.

ഞാൻ ഇവിടെ സുരക്ഷിതയും സന്തോഷവതിയും ആയതുകാരണം എന്നെ കുറിച്ച് വ്യകുലപെടാതെ എൻ്റെ മകൾ സന്തോഷവതിയായി Banglore-ൽ കഴിയുന്നു.
