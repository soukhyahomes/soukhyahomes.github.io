Special Features

Amazing Atmosphere

Situated in the middle of a panoramic surrounding. Calm ambience with singing of birds. Full campus is planted with fruit trees and green natural lawns. It is an excellent place for relaxed living.

Vehicle & Driver

Car and driver is available in the campus for emergency services. On call vehicles are also made available for any personal planned travels like airport pickup and drop, picnic etc.

Yoga for Relaxation

A place for Yoga is provided. A yoga trainer will visit occasionally.

Latest Medical Care

Daily visit by a doctor is arranged. Soukhya has signed MoU with two multi speciality hospitals. One can reach the hospitals in 15 minutes by car.

Assisted Living Support

Soukhya will arrange full time assistance for the needy inmates.

Full Time Caretaker

Caretaker is residing inside the campus for making their service always available.

Car Parking and Guest Apartment

Separate car parking for residents, visitors and guest apartments will be provided.

Entertainments

Facilities for indoor and outdoor games and a library with reading room will be provided. Mini theatre for TV is arranged and Free wifi in rooms will be provided.
