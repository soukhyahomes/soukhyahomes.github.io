Facilities at Soukhya

Accommodation is provided in Luxury Studio Apartments / Villas with fully furnished air-conditioned bedroom with twin bed, furnished drawing room and kitchenette with refrigerator, microwave oven and kettle, toilet with hot water and grab bar and balcony with full view of luscious greenery.

Fully landscaped yard with walkway, an organic fruit garden, fish pond, parking area for residents, restaurant like dining area and food of your choice, well equipped fitness centre with separate zone for yoga and meditation, library & reading room, indoor and outdoor game zones, doctor & clinic, ayurveda wellness centre, gossip zone, laundry services and round the clock security.

Daycare Facility

Soukhya provides daycare facility for parents of employees who do not want to keep their parents alone at home when they go employment.

Ayurveda Wellness centre

An Ayurvedic wellness centre is functioning in the campus for providing services of trained ayurvedic practioners and doctor.

Energy Charges

Normal usage of energy is free for the inmates.

Laundry Service

Free centralized laundry service is made available to ensure the hygienic living in the villas.

Amazing atmosphere

Situated in the middle of a panoramic surrounding. Calm ambience with singing of birds. Full campus is planted with fruit trees and green natural lawns. It is an excellent place for relaxed living.
